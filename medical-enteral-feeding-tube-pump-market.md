Peristaltic pumps utilize rotating rollers to propel food through the tube, ensuring a consistent flow. Injection pumps enable precise medication delivery along with nutrition, often used in specialized cases. Electronic pumps offer advanced programming features for customizable feeding schedules and dosages, enhancing patient care. Additionally, other types may include gravity-fed systems and disposable units, catering to diverse clinical needs and preferences.</p></p>
