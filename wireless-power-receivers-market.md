<p><strong>The Wireless Power Receivers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inductive Coupling Wireless Power Receivers</li><li>Capacitive Coupling Wireless Power Receivers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireless power receivers market comprises primarily two types: inductive coupling and capacitive coupling technologies. Inductive coupling wireless power receivers utilize magnetic fields to transfer energy between coils, commonly seen in applications like smartphone charging pads. In contrast, capacitive coupling wireless power receivers operate through electric fields, allowing energy transfer between conductive plates. Both technologies cater to various applications, including consumer electronics, electric vehicles, and medical devices, enhancing convenience and supporting the growing demand for cable-free charging solutions.</p></p>
