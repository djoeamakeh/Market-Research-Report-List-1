<p><p>The Cerebrospinal Fluid Protein Determination Kit market comprises two main types: enzymatic and colorimetric methods. Enzymatic assays utilize enzymes to quantify protein levels in cerebrospinal fluid, offering high specificity and sensitivity. In contrast, colorimetric methods rely on the development of color in a reaction to measure protein concentration, providing straightforward and rapid results. Both types play crucial roles in diagnosing neurological disorders by assessing protein levels, impacting treatment decisions and patient management strategies in clinical settings.</p></p>
