<p><p>The Medical Nuclear Imaging System market encompasses various technologies for diagnosing and monitoring diseases. SPECT systems use gamma radiation to create 3D images of the body, highlighting functional processes. Hybrid PET systems combine positron emission tomography with computed tomography, providing both metabolic and anatomical information for precise diagnostics. Planar scintigraphy involves 2D imaging using gamma cameras, primarily for assessing organ function and detecting abnormalities. Each type plays a vital role in enhancing patient care through advanced imaging techniques.</p></p>
