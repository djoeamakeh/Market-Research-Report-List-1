<p><strong>The Whole Slide Scanner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Brightfield</li><li>Fluorescence</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The whole slide scanner market is characterized by two primary types: brightfield and fluorescence scanners. Brightfield scanners utilize standard white light to illuminate stained tissue samples, providing clear images for histopathological analysis. In contrast, fluorescence scanners employ specific wavelengths of light to excite fluorescent dyes, enabling the visualization of cellular components that may not be easily seen with brightfield methods. Both technologies play crucial roles in diagnostics, research, and education, enhancing the capabilities of pathologists and researchers in understanding complex biological specimens.</p></p>
