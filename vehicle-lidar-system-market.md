<p><strong>The Vehicle Lidar System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Lidar</li><li>Solid State Lidar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Vehicle Lidar System market comprises two primary types: Mechanical Lidar and Solid-State Lidar. Mechanical Lidar utilizes rotating components to scan the environment, offering high-resolution data and a wide field of view, making it suitable for advanced driver-assistance systems. In contrast, Solid-State Lidar eliminates moving parts, resulting in a more compact, durable design with reduced manufacturing costs. While Solid-State Lidar is gaining traction for its reliability and size, Mechanical Lidar remains popular for its proven performance and accuracy.</p></p>
