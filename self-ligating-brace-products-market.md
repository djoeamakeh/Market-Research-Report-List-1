<p><p>The self-ligating bracket market is categorized mainly into two types: metal self-ligating brackets and ceramic self-ligating brackets. Metal self-ligating brackets are constructed from stainless steel, offering durability and efficient tooth movement with minimal friction. They provide a more visible option. In contrast, ceramic self-ligating brackets are made from tooth-colored materials, making them more aesthetically appealing while still allowing for effective treatment. Both types enhance patient comfort and reduce the need for traditional elastic ligatures, streamlining orthodontic procedures.</p></p>
