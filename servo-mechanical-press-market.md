<p><strong>The Servo Mechanical Press Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Link-assisted"</li><li>"Direct Drive"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The servo mechanical press market is primarily divided into two main types: link-assisted and direct drive. Link-assisted presses utilize a mechanical linkage to convert the rotary motion of a servo motor into linear motion, which enhances precision and power while maintaining energy efficiency. In contrast, direct drive presses feature a servo motor directly connected to the ram, allowing for smoother operations, faster cycle times, and precise control over force and speed. Both types cater to varying industrial needs and applications.</p></p>
