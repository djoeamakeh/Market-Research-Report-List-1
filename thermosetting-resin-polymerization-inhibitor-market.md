<p><ul><li>Molecular Type</li><li>Stable Radical Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermosetting resin polymerization inhibitor market is categorized into two primary types: molecular type inhibitors and stable radical type inhibitors. Molecular type inhibitors function by chemically interacting with monomers to prevent premature polymerization, ensuring controlled curing processes. In contrast, stable radical type inhibitors work by introducing stable free radicals that interrupt the polymerization chain reaction, effectively extending the working life of the resin. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing the performance and usability of thermosetting resins in various applications.</p></p>
