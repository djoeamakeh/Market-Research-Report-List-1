<p><p>The bronchoscope video endoscopes market comprises two main types: rigid endoscopes and flexible endoscopes. Rigid endoscopes are inflexible tubes that provide clear visualization and are typically used for diagnostic and therapeutic procedures, often in surgical settings. Flexible endoscopes, on the other hand, feature a bendable design, allowing for easier navigation through the bronchial tree, making them ideal for comprehensive examinations and biopsies. Both types are essential for various respiratory treatments and diagnostics in pulmonology.</p></p>
