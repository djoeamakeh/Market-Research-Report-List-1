<p><p>The anti-idiotype antibody market is segmented into three main types. Antigen blocking anti-idiotype antibodies inhibit the interaction between an antibody and its target antigen, providing valuable therapeutic options. Non-blocking anti-idiotype antibodies, on the other hand, do not interfere with this interaction but can serve as biomarkers or diagnostic tools. Complex specific anti-idiotype antibodies specifically recognize and bind to antibody-antigen complexes, playing a crucial role in studying immune responses and potential therapies. Each type serves unique functions in immunology.</p></p>
