<p><ul><li>Comprehensive Coverage Insurance</li><li>Single Coverage Insurance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Temporary car insurance is tailored for short-term needs and typically falls into two market types: comprehensive coverage insurance and single coverage insurance. Comprehensive coverage offers broad protection, covering damages to the insured vehicle, third-party liabilities, theft, and natural disasters. In contrast, single coverage insurance focuses solely on liability, protecting against damages to other vehicles or property in an accident while excluding coverage for the insured vehicle. These options cater to varying levels of risk and financial protection for drivers.</p></p>
