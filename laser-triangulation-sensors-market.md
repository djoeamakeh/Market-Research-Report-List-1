<p><p>Laser triangulation sensors are sophisticated devices that utilize laser technology to measure distances and dimensions with high precision. These sensors emit a laser beam onto a target surface, with the reflected light captured by a detector, allowing for accurate calculations of distance based on triangulation principles. This technology is widely used in various sectors, including manufacturing, robotics, quality control, and automation processes due to its ability to provide precise measurements without contact.</p><p>The Laser Triangulation Sensors Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.10% during the forecast period.
