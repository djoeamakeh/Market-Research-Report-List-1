<p><p>The polystyrene (PS) resins market primarily includes two types: General Purpose Polystyrene (GPPS) and High Impact Polystyrene (HIPS). GPPS is a clear, rigid resin widely used in applications like packaging and consumer goods due to its excellent clarity and moldability. In contrast, HIPS is a modified version that incorporates elastomers, enhancing its impact resistance, making it suitable for products requiring durability, such as appliances and toys. Both types serve specific needs across various industries, driving market demand.</p></p>
