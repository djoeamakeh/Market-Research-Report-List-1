<p><p>The optical distance sensors market is primarily divided into two types: laser and LED sensors. Laser type sensors utilize focused beams of light to measure distances with high precision, making them ideal for applications requiring accuracy over long ranges. In contrast, LED type sensors employ light-emitting diodes for shorter distance measurements, often used in consumer electronics and safety systems. Both technologies play crucial roles in automation, robotics, and various industrial applications by enabling efficient distance measurement and object detection.</p></p>
