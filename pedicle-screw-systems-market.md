<p><p>The pedicle screw systems market is segmented into three main types: conical, cylindrical, and dual-cored pedicle screws. Conical pedicle screws offer enhanced stability and better load distribution due to their tapered design, making them suitable for varied anatomical conditions. Cylindrical pedicle screws, with their uniform shape, facilitate easier insertion and alignment in spinal surgeries. Dual-cored pedicle screws combine features of both types, providing versatility in application, improved strength, and reduced risk of screw loosening, catering to diverse surgical requirements.</p></p>
