<p><p>Optogenetic gene therapy encompasses three main market types: </p><p>1. **Light Sensitive Proteins**: These are proteins engineered to respond to light, enabling precise control of cellular activities and functions.</p><p>  </p><p>2. **Light Sensitive Protein Transporters**: These facilitate the delivery of light-sensitive proteins into target cells, enhancing the efficacy of optogenetic applications. </p><p>3. **Light Stimulation Systems**: These systems provide the necessary light sources and devices to activate the light-sensitive proteins, enabling researchers to manipulate cellular behavior in real-time for therapeutic purposes.</p></p>
