Intraoral film processors are designed for developing films taken from inside the mouth, offering high-resolution images essential for diagnosis and treatment planning. Extraoral film processors are used for films taken outside the mouth, such as panoramic or cephalometric X-rays, providing broader views for comprehensive assessments. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing dental imaging and improving patient care.</p></p>
