<p><p>Impulse wave diagnostic ultrasound devices are categorized into three main types: A Type, M Type, and B Type. A Type devices provide amplitude information for tissue structure analysis, useful in ophthalmology and cardiology. M Type systems focus on motion, delivering time-related visualization for assessing heart functions and blood flow dynamics. B Type ultrasound generates two-dimensional images for comprehensive visualizations of soft tissues and organs, aiding in diagnosis and treatment planning across various medical specialties. Each type serves distinct clinical applications and imaging needs.</p></p>
