<p><strong>The Tapered Implant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Root-form Dental Implants</li><li>Plate-form Dental Implants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tapered implant market primarily includes two types: root-form and plate-form dental implants. Root-form dental implants have a tapered design that mimics the natural tooth root, promoting osseointegration and making them suitable for various bone types. They provide stability and support in areas with limited bone density. In contrast, plate-form dental implants are wider and flatter, designed for patients with specific anatomical considerations. They offer a larger surface area for enhanced stabilization, particularly in cases where bone width is constrained.</p></p>
