<p><p>Overhead conductors are essential components in electrical transmission systems, facilitating the movement of electricity across long distances. OPGW (Optical Ground Wire) combines the functions of grounding and optical communication. Within the OPGW market, Layer Stranding Structure OPGW features multiple layers of optical fibers wound around a central core, enhancing durability and flexibility. In contrast, Central Tube Structure OPGW houses fibers within a single central tube, offering protection and ease of installation, catering to diverse applications in power and telecommunication sectors.</p></p>
