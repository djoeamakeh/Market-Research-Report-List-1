<p><ul><li>Cold Chain Logistics</li><li>Non-cold Chain Logistics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pharmaceutical logistics encompasses two primary market types: cold chain logistics and non-cold chain logistics. Cold chain logistics involves the transportation and storage of temperature-sensitive products, such as vaccines and biologics, requiring strict temperature control to maintain efficacy. In contrast, non-cold chain logistics pertains to the distribution of pharmaceutical products that do not necessitate temperature regulation, such as tablets and capsules. Efficient management of both logistics types is crucial to ensure product integrity, regulatory compliance, and timely delivery within the healthcare supply chain.</p></p>
