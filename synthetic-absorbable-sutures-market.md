<p><p>Synthetic absorbable sutures are medical devices made from synthetic materials that are designed to facilitate wound closure while gradually dissolving in the body over time. These sutures eliminate the need for removal, making them particularly advantageous in procedures where healing is expected, such as in soft tissue repairs or in pediatric surgeries. Their ease of use and reduced risk of infection contribute to their increasing popularity among healthcare professionals.</p><p>The Synthetic Absorbable Sutures Market is projected to grow at a CAGR of 7.1% during the forecast period, driven by factors such as the rising number of surgical procedures globally, innovations in suture materials, and the increasing preference for minimally invasive surgeries. The growing elderly population, coupled with an increase in chronic health conditions requiring surgical intervention, is also contributing to market expansion.
