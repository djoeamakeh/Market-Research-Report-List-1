<p><p>The light mining dump truck market comprises two main types: articulated trucks and rigid trucks. Articulated trucks feature a hinge connecting the cab and the trailer, allowing for improved maneuverability in tight spaces, making them ideal for uneven terrain. Rigid trucks, on the other hand, have a fixed chassis, providing greater load capacity and stability on smoother roads. Both types are essential for efficient transportation of materials in mining operations, offering distinct advantages depending on the specific site conditions and requirements.</p></p>
