<p><p>The pharmaceutical packaging materials market encompasses various adhesive types tailored for securing packaging components. Solvent adhesives utilize organic solvents to dissolve polymers, ensuring strong bonds but may pose environmental and health concerns. Solvent-free adhesives eliminate solvents altogether, promoting safer applications with lower volatile organic compound emissions. Waterborne adhesives, composed primarily of water and polymers, offer an eco-friendly alternative with easy cleanup and low toxicity, making them increasingly popular in pharmaceutical packaging applications where safety and compliance are critical.</p></p>
