<p><ul><li>Virus</li><li>For Antibodies</li><li>For Antigens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hepatitis B testing is categorized into three main types: testing for the virus, antibodies, and antigens. Virus testing directly detects the presence of the hepatitis B virus (HBV) in the blood, indicating active infection. Antibody tests identify the immune response to HBV, indicating whether someone has been infected previously or vaccinated. Antigen testing measures the surface proteins of the virus, helping to determine active infection and infectivity. Each type plays a crucial role in diagnosing and managing hepatitis B.</p></p>
