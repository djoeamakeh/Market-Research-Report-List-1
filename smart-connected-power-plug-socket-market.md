<p><p>The Smart-Connected Power Plug Socket market primarily comprises two types: Wi-Fi and Bluetooth. Wi-Fi-enabled sockets connect directly to the home network, allowing remote control and automation through smartphones and smart home systems. While generally easier to set up, they have a limited range, making them suitable for localized use.</p></p>
