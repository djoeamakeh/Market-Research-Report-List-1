<p><p>The lens array market is segmented into one-dimensional and two-dimensional types based on the arrangement of lens elements. One-dimensional lens arrays consist of a linear configuration, often used in applications like beam shaping and optical communication, where light needs to be directed in a single axis. In contrast, two-dimensional lens arrays feature a grid-like layout, enabling more complex light manipulation for applications like imaging, display technologies, and photonic devices, allowing for greater versatility and enhanced optical performance.</p></p>
