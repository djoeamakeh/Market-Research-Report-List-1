<p><p>Entrance card markets can be categorized into two main types: contact and contactless. Contact type cards require physical contact with a card reader to function, often using magnetic strips or traditional RFID technology. In contrast, contactless type cards utilize advanced technologies like NFC and RFID, allowing users to simply tap or wave their card near a reader for access. This convenience promotes quicker entry and enhances user experience, making contactless cards increasingly popular in various applications, including security and payment systems.</p></p>
