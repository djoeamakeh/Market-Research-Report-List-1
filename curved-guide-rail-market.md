<p><p>The curved guide rail market consists of three primary types: linear parts, curve parts, and 360-degree loop parts. Linear parts are designed to facilitate straight movements while providing stability. Curve parts enable smooth transitions between different angles, enhancing navigability. Meanwhile, 360-degree loop parts allow for complete circular movements, often utilized in amusement rides or complex machinery. Each type plays a crucial role in various applications, ensuring safety and operational efficiency in systems requiring guided motion.</p></p>
