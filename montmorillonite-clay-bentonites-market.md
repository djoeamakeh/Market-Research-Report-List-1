<p><p>Montmorillonite clay, commonly known as bentonite, is categorized into three main types based on its dominant cation: sodium bentonite, calcium bentonite, and potassium bentonite. Sodium bentonite, with high swelling capacity, is often used in drilling and environmental applications. Calcium bentonite, characterized by its low swelling properties, is utilized in construction and as a binding agent. Potassium bentonite is less common but valued for its nutrient content, making it suitable for agricultural applications and soil improvement. Each type serves distinct industrial needs.</p></p>
