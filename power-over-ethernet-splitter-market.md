<p><strong>The Power over Ethernet Splitter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive</li><li>Active</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Power over Ethernet (PoE) splitter market is divided into two main types: passive and active. </p><p>Passive PoE splitters deliver power without any conversion or negotiation, functioning solely based on the electrical signals received. They are simple and cost-effective but may lack versatility. </p><p>Active PoE splitters, on the other hand, convert power to various voltage levels and offer features like power management and status monitoring. They provide greater flexibility and compatibility with diverse devices, making them suitable for more complex installations.</p></p>
