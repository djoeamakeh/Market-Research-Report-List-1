<p><p>The screen printing machine market is primarily divided into two types: manual and automatic screen printing machines. Manual screen printing machines require operators to manually position screens and apply ink, making them suitable for small-scale or custom jobs. In contrast, automatic screen printing machines use advanced technology to automate the printing process, enhancing speed and efficiency, making them ideal for large production runs. Both types cater to diverse industries, including apparel, signage, and packaging, fulfilling varying production needs.</p></p>
