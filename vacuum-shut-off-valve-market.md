High Vacuum Valves function effectively in the range of 10^-3 to 10^-7 mbar, ideal for scientific and industrial uses that require improved vacuum conditions. Ultra-high Vacuum Valves operate below 10^-7 mbar, critical for research and specialized applications demanding extreme vacuum levels.</p></p>
