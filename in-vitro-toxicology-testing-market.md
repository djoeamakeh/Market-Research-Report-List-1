High-throughput technology allows rapid testing of multiple compounds, enhancing efficiency. Cellular imaging technology enables visualization of cellular responses to toxins at a microscopic level, aiding in analysis. Toxicogenomics integrates genomics with toxicology, examining gene expression changes due to toxic substances, facilitating a deeper understanding of toxic mechanisms.</p></p>
